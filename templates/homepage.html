Dudley People's School for Climate Justice is an assemblage of community-led projects. The projects make it easier for people to learn from each other and connect with nature. Everyone is a teacher, and everyone is a learner. Using nature’s guidebook, place-based peer learning will lead to action for people, planet and the future.
